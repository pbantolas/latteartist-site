Latte Art: Tracked. Improved.

Tired of scattered photos and guesswork in your latte art practice? LatteArtist is a simple iOS journal designed to help you intentionally log pours, track techniques, and clearly visualize your improvement over time. Developed with feedback from the home barista community on Reddit.

  • Capture each pour with photo and notes
  • Track progress by pattern (e.g., tulips over time)
  • Rate pours to reflect how you felt about them
  • Filter by cup, milk type, pattern, and more
  • Share progress collages (Planned!)
